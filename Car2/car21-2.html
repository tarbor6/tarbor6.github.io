The AV (atrioventricular) node gives rise to the left and right bundle branches, which conduct impulses that coordinate contraction of the left and right ventricles. These branches are not visible in this individual.

Make certain to find some time to review the cardiac conduction system using the reconstructed models, plastinates, and anatomical illustrations.

Briefly trace the path of blood flow from the superior and inferior vena cava through the tricuspid valve, into the right ventricle and out through the pulmonic valve into the pulmonary arteries.

Spend additional time exploring the flow of blood through the heart and associated vessels to solidify your understanding of this circulatory pathway.
