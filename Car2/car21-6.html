Now that you know the anatomical positions of the heart valves, why is the stethoscope placed over areas of the chest wall that differ from the valve location when auscultating valve sounds?

The stethoscope is placed in areas where the heart sounds are best transmitted through the chest wall. The heart sounds that you hear are vibrations traveling with the flow of blood.
