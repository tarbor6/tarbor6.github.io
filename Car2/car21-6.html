Knowing the the anatomical locations of these valves, why is the stethescope placed on the chest in areas different than where the valves are actually located?

The heart sounds that you hear are vibrations that travel with the flow of blood. Therefore you listen to heart sounds down stream from where the valves are actually located. See the below drawing to understand phenommenon.
