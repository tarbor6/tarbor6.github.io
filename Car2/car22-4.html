Add the pericardium, the membranous sac that encloses the heart.
Remove the heart to see the pericardium in isolation.

What are the three components of the pericardium?

Fibrous pericardium - the tough outer connective tissue layer

Parietal layer of the serous pericardium - lines the inner surface of the fibrous pericardium

Visceral layer of the serous pericardium (epicardium) - adheres directly to the surface of the heart
Add the diaphragm and lungs to provide spatial context for the position of the pericardium within the thoracic cavity.
