The anterior diaphragm attaches to the xyphoid process at what thoracic vertebral level?

Typically, the xiphisternal joint (and thus its muscle attachments) are around the T9 vertebral level.

At what vertebral levels does the diaphragm attach to the vertebral bodies?

Typically, the right crus attaches to the L1-L3 lumbar vertebral bodies and the left crus attaches to L1-L2.
