Examine the mesovarium, the portion of the broad ligament that suspends the ovary and conveys the ovarian vessels.
View the mesosalpinx, the superior portion of the broad ligament that encloses the uterine (fallopian) tubes.
Identify the mesometrium, the largest portion of the broad ligament that supports the uterus itself.
Add the urinary bladder and highlight the ureters.
